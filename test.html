Government Incentives

Many governments are implementing subsidies and tax breaks. Federal incentives in the US are expected to increase renewable adoption by 25% over the next five years. You can check the latest programs at Department of Energy.

Environmental Impact

One of the most important benefits of renewable energy is its effect on the environment. Switching to renewables could cut global carbon emissions by up to 40% by 2050. Scientists emphasize that every fraction of reduction helps combat climate change.
